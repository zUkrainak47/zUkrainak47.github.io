Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to UkraTimer as published on this site.

Open Timer Terms of Service

What the app stores

UkraTimer stores your timer data in your browser on your device. This may include solve times, sessions, comments, settings, layout preferences, and similar timer-related data.

This local data is stored using browser features such as localStorage and IndexedDB so the timer can keep working between visits and, in some cases, offline.

Google Drive backup

If you choose to use the optional Google Drive backup feature, the app will request permission to create, update, download, and delete its own app data in your Google Drive.

The app uses that permission only to save and restore your UkraTimer backup data. It does not use it to read your general Google Drive files.

Backups are stored in Google Drive under Google’s systems and are also subject to Google’s own privacy terms and policies.

Analytics

This site may use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate usage, performance, and reliability. That analytics service is configured on the site itself and is intended to provide high-level traffic information rather than personal timer data.

Data sharing

UkraTimer does not sell your personal information. Timer data is primarily stored locally in your own browser, and optional cloud backups are stored in your Google account only when you choose to enable them.

Your choices

  • You can clear local timer data from your browser by using your browser’s site data controls.
  • You can stop using Google Drive backup at any time by disconnecting the app from Google in the timer UI or in your Google account permissions.
  • You can delete cloud backup data through the app’s Google backup actions or through your Google account, where available.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time as the app changes. Updates will be posted on this page.
